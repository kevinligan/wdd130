Overview

Purpose

The Rafting Site Plan website serves as an informative platform, offering essential details and guidance for enthusiasts and adventurers seeking to explore the best rafting destinations, safety tips, and trip planning resources.

Audience

The Rafting Site Plan website caters primarily to adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and rafting aficionados of all experience levels, providing valuable information and resources to help them plan and enjoy safe and memorable rafting adventures.

Who are the target customers? The target customers for the Rafting Site Plan website are adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and rafting aficionados of various ages, from young adults to middle-aged individuals, with a range of financial means, interested in outdoor activities and river adventures.

What do they want? They want valuable information, resources, and guidance on planning and enjoying safe and memorable rafting adventures. They seek trip details, safety tips, gear recommendations, and expert advice to enhance their experience.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The audience will primarily access the site through mobile devices, given the on-the-go nature of outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers. Some may also use laptops and desktops for more detailed planning.
